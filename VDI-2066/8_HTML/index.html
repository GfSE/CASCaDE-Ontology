    ○  Energy

    The energy flow in and out of the system, here in its most general form. Should be specialized to its actual form in an application, such as electrical, mechanical or other energy.

    Definition: In physics, power is the amount of energy transferred or converted per unit time. In the International System of Units, the unit of power is the watt, equal to one joule per second. Power is a scalar quantity. Power is the rate with respect to time at which work is done; it is the time derivative of work. Source: Wikipedia.
